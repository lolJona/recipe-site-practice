Salsa!

Description:

Salsa is dang delicious. It just can't be beat!

Wanna know how to make salsa? Here's what you'll need:

  • Tomatoes
  • Tomatillos
  • 9 Onions
  • Some Garlic
  • Sweet music
  • 30 limes

Steps:

  1. Open your tomatoes.
  2. Chop onions.
  3. Add limes.
  4. Mix.
  5. Enjoy!
